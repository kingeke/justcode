# Classifications of Foods

| Category | Examples | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Fruits | Apples, Bananas, Berries | Sweet or tart, high in vitamins and fiber |
| Vegetables | Carrots, Broccoli, Leafy greens | Often low-calorie, nutrient dense |
| Grains | Rice, Bread, Pasta | Provide carbohydrates; can be whole‑grain or refined |
| Protein | Chicken, Tofu, Lentils | Essential for muscle repair and growth |
| Dairy & Alternatives | Milk, Cheese, Almond milk | Source of calcium, can vary in fat content |
| Fats & Oils | Olive oil, Butter, Avocado | Provide energy; unsaturated vs saturated fats |
| Sweeteners | Sugar, Honey, Artificial sweeteners | Added calories or zero‑calorie substitutes |

## Other Classifications
- **Processed vs. Unprocessed** – Refers to the extent chemicals, heat, or other treatments have been applied.
- **Organic vs. Conventional** – Farming practices and pesticide use.
- **Gluten Content** – Gluten‑free, gluten‑containing.
- **Allergenicity** – Contains common allergens such as nuts, soy, dairy, eggs, wheat, fish, shellfish.
